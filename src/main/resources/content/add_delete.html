Add / Delete speed

Controls how important fast insertion and removal are.

HashMap and HashSet are usually O(1). TreeMap and TreeSet are O(log n).

ArrayDeque is very efficient at both ends.

ArrayList is fast at the end, but inserting/removing in the middle is O(n).

Set this high if your program frequently:

  • Adds items
  • Removes items
  • Pushes / pops
  • Enqueues / dequeues
